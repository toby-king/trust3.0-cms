The Process

Strategic and customer experience design sprints to develop a core model of the customer data journey

Understand the broad needs of the data ecosystem.

Define principles and a value exchange model that rewrites the rules on data ownership and usage, leading to target personas.

Create a customer experience data model via a series of 3 experiences, in the Metaverse and Physically, to prove out the model

Test the journeys with key stakeholders to agree on the new formula

Create a CX data blueprint model that serves as a framework for all new programmes

What We Deliver

A series of principles and blueprints for data-driven best practices that enable a framework for your technology ecosystem.

  1. Guiding principles for how you deliver experiences and services for your customers.
  2. Data Specification model for your customers that delivers a sovereign model.
  3. Blueprints in the form of data-driven user journeys that embody the principles, the model, and deliver a cognitive-enabled ecosystem.

These will enable you to design and develop a Technology Framework that deliver a strategy based on trust.
