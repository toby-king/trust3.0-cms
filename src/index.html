Our Partners

Without privacy there is no freedom of thought

In the last two decades, the tech industry has moved too fast and broken too many things. Every day we all make seemingly insignificant concessions, like agreeing to Ts & Cs we never read, and share our Personal Identifiable Information without second thought.

Striving to create a cross-industry dialogue that fosters innovation

We strive to create a safe and open environment for industry experts to come together and share their experiences. Convening the brightest minds in privacy, AI, and technology to foster a global dialogue that champions responsible innovation for a safer society.

Our Mission

To create a safer digital environment for all, where individuals can exercise their right to privacy and security online without fear of exploitation or intrusion. We aim to acheieve this by helping to raise awareness and promote online privacy by engaging directly with brands to develop and improve consumer privacy protections and coordinate efforts to safeguard personal data by offering accessible and comprehensive resources.
